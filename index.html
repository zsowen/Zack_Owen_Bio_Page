Zack Owen Bio

Zack Owen is a Richmond based Magician and Theatre Director. By day he works in the thrilling
world of finance, helping people acheive their mortgage goals. By night he is a director in the
Richmond theatre community. He has directed six full length productions in the Richmond
community, working with theatres such as HATTheatre and CAT Theatre as well as founding
Wishing Wind Creations.

He has also been performing Magic since the early days of High School. While he doesn't work as a
magician professionally often, he is passionate about bringing that sense of wonder to those around
him. He is in the process of building a one man magic show that is both an enchanting evening of
illusion, and an emotional journey into the trials and tribulations of his life.
